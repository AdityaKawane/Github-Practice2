Situated in Bhopal, Hotel Aditya Residency features 4-star accommodation with private balconies. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, room service and a 24-hour front desk, along with free WiFi throughout the property. Private parking is available on site. At the hotel rooms are equipped with air conditioning, a desk, a terrace with a city view, a private bathroom, a flat-screen TV, bed linen and towels. The rooms will provide guests with a wardrobe and a coffee machine. Museum of Man is 4.3 km from Hotel Aditya Residency, while Habibganj Station is 4.3 km from the property. The nearest airport is Raja Bhoj Airport, 10 km from the accommodation
